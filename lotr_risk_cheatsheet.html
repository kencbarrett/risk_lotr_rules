RISK: LORD OF THE RINGS - QUICK REFERENCE

Quick Rules Overview

Players: 2-4 | Age: 10+

Objective

Score points by controlling territories, regions, and completing missions. Don't let the Fellowship reach Mount Doom!

The 8 Steps of Your Turn

⚠️ STEP 1 MUST BE DONE FIRST: Reinforce Strongholds (often missed!)

  1. Receive and place reinforcements (always)
    1. 1a. Reinforce Strongholds - Place 1 battalion into EACH territory with a stronghold you control (THIS IS FIRST!)
    2. 1b. Count Territories - Divide by 3 (ignore remainder), minimum 3 reinforcements
    3. 1c. Add Region Bonuses - Check gameboard chart for each complete region you control
    4. 1d. Turn In Card Sets - If you have 5+ cards, you MUST turn in a set (see Card Sets table below)
    5. 1e. Place ALL Reinforcements - Place everything gathered above into your territories
  2. Combat (invade other territories - optional)
  3. Fortify your position (optional - ONE fortification move allowed)
  4. Collect a territory card (if you conquered at least 1 territory)
  5. Collect an adventure card (if your Leader conquered a territory with a Site of Power)
  6. Replace a leader (if you have no Leaders on the board)
  7. Try to find the Ring (EVIL only - see Finding the Ring section below)
  8. Move the Fellowship (see Fellowship Movement section below)

Combat Rules

  • Attacking Requirements: Need at least 2 battalions in territory to attack (1 must stay behind to protect territory)
  • Attacking: Send 1-3 battalions per battle (attacker rolls 1, 2, or 3 dice)
  • Defending: Defend with 1-2 battalions per battle (defender rolls 1 or 2 dice)
  • Resolving: Compare highest die to highest die, then next-highest to next-highest
  • Tie Breaker: Defender wins ties
  • Continue: Repeat battles until territory is conquered or invasion is called off

Combat Bonuses

Condition Bonus
Leader attacking +1 to highest attack die
Leader defending +1 to higher defense die
Stronghold defending +1 to higher defense die
Leader + Stronghold defending +2 to higher defense die

Important: Bonuses are only added to the highest/higher die roll, not to all dice. Leader bonuses apply only when the Leader is in combat.

Strongholds & Sites of Power

Strongholds:

  • Place 1 battalion into each stronghold territory at the START of your turn (Step 1a)
  • Provide +1 to higher defense die when defending
  • Worth 2 points at game end (scoring)

Sites of Power:

  • Worth 2 points at game end, but only if you control the entire region
  • Conquering a Site of Power with a Leader allows you to draw an Adventure card

Leaders

  • Movement: Leaders move WITH battalions (cannot move alone)
  • Combat: Add +1 to highest attack die or higher defense die (only when in combat)
  • After Conquering: If Leader was used to conquer a territory, the Leader must move into that territory
  • Leader Elimination: If the last battalion in a territory with a Leader is defeated, the Leader is removed
  • Passing Through: A Leader can pass through a territory containing your other Leader, but cannot end in the same territory
  • Replacement: If you have no Leaders on the board, place one in any of your territories (Step 6)

Adventure Cards

  • Mission Cards: Complete by getting your Leader to the Site of Power listed on the card. Keep completed cards for scoring.
  • Event Cards: Say "Play Immediately" - must be played when drawn. See Event Card Handling below.
  • Power Cards: Play during combat (or sometimes during other players' turns) for advantages. Keep played cards for scoring.

Event Card Handling (Step 5)

When you draw an Adventure card after conquering a Site of Power:

  1. If it's an Event card (says "Play Immediately"), play it immediately
  2. Draw another Adventure card
  3. Continue playing Event cards and drawing until you get a Mission or Power card
  4. Add the Mission or Power card to your hand
  5. If you now have more than 4 Adventure cards, discard one (your choice)

Important: After a Mission or Power card is drawn, no more Adventure cards can be played that turn.

Scoring Points

Item Points
Each territory controlled 1 point
Each stronghold controlled 2 points
Each complete region controlled Equal to that region's battalion bonus (check gameboard chart)
Each Site of Power controlled 2 points (only if you control the entire region)
Adventure cards played Points indicated on the card (cards in hand don't count)

Note: Only Adventure cards that have been played (Mission cards completed, Power cards used) count for scoring. Cards still in your hand do not count.

Finding the Ring (Step 7 - EVIL Only)

When: Just before moving the Fellowship (Step 8), if The One Ring is in a territory controlled by an evil player.

  • Roll: The evil player who controls the territory rolls 2 dice (any color)
  • Bonuses:
    • +1 if controlling the entire region where the Ring is located
    • +1 if your Leader is in the territory with the Ring
    • +2 if both conditions (control entire region AND Leader present)
  • Success: If the total (dice roll + bonuses) is 12 or higher, The One Ring is found and evil instantly wins!

Moving the Fellowship (Step 8)

Normal Movement: Move The One Ring (Fellowship) 1 territory along the dotted path.

DIE Symbol Territories: If the Ring is currently on a territory with a DIE symbol:

  • You MUST roll 1 die before moving
  • Roll > 3: Move to the next territory on the path
  • Roll ≤ 3: The Fellowship stays in the current territory (try again next turn)
  • The Fellowship will remain in this territory until a successful roll is made

Winning the Game

When the Fellowship reaches Mount Doom:

  • Make a final die roll to destroy The One Ring
  • Roll > 3: The One Ring is destroyed - game ends, calculate final scores
  • Roll ≤ 3: The Fellowship has not destroyed The One Ring - game continues
  • Each player, at the end of their turn, rolls to destroy The One Ring until successful
  • Alternative Win: Evil can win by finding The One Ring (see Finding the Ring above)

Winner: Player with the highest score wins!
